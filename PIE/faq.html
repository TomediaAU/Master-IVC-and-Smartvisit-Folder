General

Q: Are any group purchase, concession card holder or family discounts available?

A: As the four types of PIE passes are already discounted no further reductions are available.

Q: Can I visit the different attractions on separate days?

A: Yes, you can visit the attractions on the days that suit you, with up to 12 months to use the pass.

Q: Can I revisit the attractions for free?

A: No, the PIE Pass only allows for you to visit your selected attractions once.

Q: Can I get pickup up from my accommodation?

A: At the moment we do not currently offer pickup services. However, there is a local taxi service, hire cars and e-bikes available on Phillip Island.

Q: Is the PIE Pass the same as the Nature Parks “3 Park Pass”?

A: No

Q: Is each attraction Pram and Wheelchair friendly?

A: Most are, however, some areas in the attractions do cause minor difficulties please check directly with the attraction you choose to visit.

In regard to any cruises, wheelchair access is unavailable due to jetty stairs

Q: If I have an issue with my booking what Phone Number can I call?

A: 0433 164 300
(If phone is unattened please leave a message and we will return your call)

Q: Where can I hire a car on Phillip Island?

A: Cars can be hired through Phillip Island Car hire located at – Anderson St Boat Ramp Kiosk, Cowes
www.phillipislandcarhire.com.au

Q: Where can we board our cats/dogs on Phillip Island ?

A: Phillip Island Kennels and Cattery
cowesboardingkennels.com.au

Bookings/Tickets

Q: When is my ticket valid from?

A: Your ticket is valid from the date of purchase

Q: How long is my ticket valid for?

A: All tickets are valid for 12 months

Q: what are the child ages?

A: Ages 4-15 yrs

Q: what if my child is under 3?

A: If they are 3 and under, they will have free entry the PIE attractions. attractions.

In regard to any cruises, please contact Wildlife Coast Cruises to add an infant to your booking. In regard to Clip ‘N Climb all climbers are required to be at least 15 kilograms.

Q: If I have any problems with my booking who should I contact?

A: Either the person that issued you the PIE pass or our customer service info@phillipislandexperience.com.au

Cape Cruise (Wildlife Coast Cruises)

Q: Do I need a Boarding Pass?

Once you have purchased your PIE pass and booked for your selected time/date you will be issued with an e-ticket confirmation, this is all you will require on boarding.

NOTE: It is important to contact Wildlife Coast Cruises on 1300 763 739 or info@wildlifecoastcruises.com.au if you have any infants in your group, as capacity is limited.

Q: Where does the Seal Cruise Depart?

A: San Remo Jetty, 170 Marine Parade Victoria for more information visit the locations page

Q: What is include in the Seals & PIE Pass and Penguins Seals & PIE Pass for Wild Life Coast Cruises?

A: 1 hour Cape Cruise

Q: Approximately how long will those activities take?

A: 1 hour, please arrive 30 mins prior to departure

Q: when do these Cape Cruises go?

A: 10:45am Sun, Tues, Wed, Fri (Extra times during peak season) www.wildlifecoastcruises.com.au/seal-watching-cruise/ for more information

Q: Do I have to pre book beforehand?

A: Yes, this is essential as there is limited availability please follow the instructions after purchasing your ticket.

Q: Will the cruise always run?

A: Cape cruises will run the vast majority of the time however they are subject to weather conditions and minimum numbers. If you have any questions in regard to this, please contact Wildlife Coast Cruises.

Q: Where do the cruise run from?

A: San Remo Jetty, located at 170 Marine Parade San Remo

Q: What bonus offers are there for the PIE pass?
