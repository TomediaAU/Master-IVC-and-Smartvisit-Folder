General

Q: How can we obtain individual passes/tickets?

A: For individual passes/tickets please purchase all your tickets separately when booking.

Q: Are any bulk purchase or concession card holder discounts available?

A: As the four types of PIE passes are already discounted no further reductions are available.

Q: Can I visit the different attractions on separate days?

A: Yes, you can visit the attractions on the days that suit you

Q: Can I revisit the attractions for free?

A: No, the PIE Pass only allows for you to visit your selected attractions once

Q: Can I get pickup up from my accommodation?

A: At the moment we do not currently offer pickup services, however if you visit www.bestof.com.au there are tours that do

Q: Is the PIE Pass the same as the Nature Parks “3 Park Pass”?

A: No

Q: Is each attraction Pram and Wheelchair friendly?

A: Most are, however some areas in the attractions do cause minor difficulties please check with the attractions you choose to visit.

Q: If I have an issue with my booking what Phone Number can I call?

A: 0433 164 300
(If phone is unattened please leave a message and we will return your call)

Q: Where can I hire a car on Phillip Island?

A: Cars can be hired through Phillip Island Car hire located at – Anderson St Boat Ramp Kiosk, Cowes
www.phillipislandcarhire.com.au

Q: Where can we board our cats/dogs on Phillip Island ?

Location

Q: Where is Phillip Island Located?

A: Phillip Island is located approximately 2 hours South from Melbourne in Australia

Q: How far is Phillip Island from Melbourne?

A: approximately 2 hours via car

Q: How would I get to Phillip Island from the Mornington Peninsula?

A: Either head east and get to Phillip Island via the South Gippsland freeway or take the passenger ferry from stony point to cowes but there is no car ferry to Phillip Island

Q: Is there any public transport to and around Phillip Island?

A: Yes, there is from Melborune and also a local bus route, however not all our attraction locations are accessible with these routes visit www.viclink.com.au for more info

Q: Where can I get more information about Phillip Island?

A: www.visitphillipisland.com

Q: What do I need to pickup my online purchased PIE Pass?

A: Preferably a printed out copy of the email receipt, digital versions are also acceptable

Q: When is my ticket valid from?

A: From the date selected on our booking system or if retail the date of purchase

Q: How long is my ticket valid for?

A: 12 months

Q: What are these start/finish dates I see on my online booking, are they relevant?

A: The start/finish dates just record the day your PIE pass starts, the tickets are valid and usable anytime in the 12 months on and after those dates

Q: what are the child ages?

A: Ages 4-15 yrs

Q: what if my child is under 4?

A: If they are under 4 years old they are free

Q: If I have any problems with my booking who should I contact?

A: Either the person that issued you the PIE pass or our customer service info@phillipislandexperience.com.au

Q: Where is Wild Life Coast Cruises located?

A: 11-13 The Esplanade Cowes, Victoria for more information visit the locations page

Q: What is include in the Seals & PIE Pass and Penguins Seals & PIE Pass for Wild Life Coast Cruises?

Q: Approximately how long will those activities take?

A: 2 hours

Q: when do these Seal Cruises go?

A: Generally Daily at 2pm, depending on the time of year check www.wildlifecoastcruises.com.au/seal-watching-cruise/ for more information

Q: Do I have to pre book beforehand?

A: Yes it is recommended but not essential and is highly recommended in the summer months(December, January)

A: No, the cruises are weather dependant and in winter sometimes passenger dependant, So it would be advised to contact Wild Life Coast Cruises close to the dates you want to go, you can call them on 1300 763 739

Q: Where do the cruise run from?

A: Cowes jetty, located on Phillip Island
